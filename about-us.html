WELCOME TO JASWANT SWEETS

Our Leadership

“ Shri Chunilal Chaudhari and Shri Inderal Chaudhari , Two brothers , laid the foundation of Jaswant Sweets in January 2004. A tiny outlet then has blossomed into sweetheart of town that serves delicacies of every taste through its present size of a large store or may we call it a Sweet Mall. Shri Chunilalll Came to Kilhapur in 2003 and started working for Panchavati Sweet mart. His urge to start own Sweet shop came into reality in 2004 when he and his younger brother Inderlall opend this tiny shop in Rajarampuri. Ever since it has evolved into a trusted brand for its variety of sweets complemented with quality and timely availability. The back end of the store, that is actual making of Sweets, Snacks & Nankeen, is managed by Shri Chunilal. He has a keen eye for quality – quantity, right from procurement of raw material to processing to packaging. The front end of the store is managed by Shri Inderlall who ensures smooth movement of serving and delivery to customers. His specialty being quick turnaround of ready to sell sweets to retain best quality from shelf to home of customer.”


JASWANT SWEETS

Best Services

Jaswant is a popular spot in Rajarampuri, known for its delicious sweets, snacks, and namkeen. From morning to night, it serves fresh, flavorful treats that delight all ages. Starting as a dine-in place, it now offers takeaways and gift options, always ensuring variety, quality, and customer satisfaction.
