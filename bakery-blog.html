Our Bakery Specialties

While we offer a wide range of bakery products, these three have become customer favorites over the years:

Khari Biscuit

Our signature flaky, puff pastry biscuits that are light as air with a satisfying crunch. Made with pure ghee and a secret folding technique that creates hundreds of delicate layers.

Perfect with: Masala chai, filter coffee, or as a base for chaat

Nankhatai

The classic Indian shortbread cookie with our special touch - a blend of besan and maida that creates the perfect crumbly texture, flavored with cardamom and nutmeg.

Perfect with: Irani chai, milk, or as a sweet ending to meals

Namak Para

Our savory diamond-cut crackers seasoned with cumin and black pepper - not too hard, not too soft, with just the right amount of saltiness to keep you reaching for more.

Perfect with: Evening tea, tomato soup, or as a cocktail snack

The Khari Biscuit Secret

Our Khari biscuits have developed a cult following in Kolhapur. Here's why they stand out:

The Dough

Made with premium maida and pure ghee in a specific ratio that creates flakiness without heaviness. The dough is kneaded by hand to just the right consistency.

The Folding

Our bakers perform a precise 7-fold technique that creates 128 layers - enough for perfect flakiness but not so many that the biscuit becomes tough.

The Baking

Baked at 180°C for exactly 22 minutes - hot enough to puff up the layers but gentle enough to prevent burning.

The Cooling

Left to cool gradually on marble slabs, which helps maintain the crisp texture without becoming brittle.

Pro Tip from Our Bakers

"To restore crispness to day-old Khari, warm it in a preheated oven at 160°C for 3 minutes - it will taste freshly baked again. Never microwave bakery items as it makes them tough."
