Healthy Mithai? Yes!

Jaswant Sweets' Sugar-Free Collection - Indulge Without Guilt

May 15, 2025

At Jaswant Sweets, we believe everyone deserves to enjoy traditional Indian sweets, regardless of dietary restrictions. Our sugar-free collection has been carefully crafted to deliver authentic flavors using natural sweeteners and premium ingredients, making them perfect for diabetics and health-conscious sweet lovers alike.

"Our sugar-free sweets aren't just about removing sugar - they're about recreating the complete mithai experience with natural alternatives that are kind to your body without compromising on taste." - Chef Priya Jaswant

Our Signature Sugar-Free Collection

1. Sugar-Free Malai Barfi

Creamy, rich malai barfi made with low-fat milk solids and naturally sweetened with erythritol. A guilt-free indulgence with every bite.

2. Sugar-Free Ladoo

Traditional ladoo flavor recreated with a sugar-free twist using natural dates and nuts. A power-packed sweet ideal for health-conscious snackers.

3. Sugar-Free Gulab Jamun

Classic Indian dessert reinvented! Soft jamuns soaked in a stevia-sweetened syrup that offers indulgence without the sugar spike.

Nutritional Benefits

Our sugar-free sweets aren't just about what they lack (refined sugar), but what they provide:

  • High in Protein: Nuts and dairy provide essential amino acids
  • Rich in Fiber: Helps regulate digestion and blood sugar
  • Healthy Fats: From nuts and pure ghee for sustained energy
  • Antioxidants: From natural ingredients like cardamom and nuts
  • Essential Minerals: Calcium, magnesium and zinc from dairy and nuts
