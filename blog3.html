Diwali Faral: A 100-Year Tradition

Explore how our family has prepared the same Diwali faral items since 1923, using the original copper utensils and techniques passed down through generations. From crunchy chaklis to perfectly sweet karanjis, every item reflects our commitment to preserving tradition. Each year, we begin preparations a month in advance, with the entire family joining in. The aroma of roasting besan, ghee, and dry fruits fills our home and shop. Our faral hampers are a bestseller during this time, filled with classics and a few modern innovations like chocolate-coated laddoos. It’s not just about taste—it's about legacy, warmth, and togetherness.

The Art of Perfect Modak

Learn why our Ukadiche Modak stays soft for 3 days while others harden—the secret lies in the rice flour preparation technique we've perfected over decades. We use a specific Kolhapuri rice, hand-pounded and double-sieved, ensuring the softest dough. The coconut-jaggery filling is made fresh each morning, enhanced with cardamom and a hint of nutmeg. Steaming is done in traditional brass steamers, ensuring even heat distribution. Customers return year after year, not just for the taste, but for the emotion that each bite of our modak carries—nostalgia, devotion, and tradition.

Beat the Heat: Summer Sweet Guide

Our seasonal recommendations: Try our chilled Aamras (mango pulp), Shrikhand with seasonal fruits, and Kala Jamun ice cream sandwiches. During the scorching Kolhapur summers, we offer specially curated sweets to cool you down. Our Mango Barfi is made with Alphonso puree and saffron, while our chilled Malai Peda boxes are perfect gifts. We also introduced Matka Mishti Doi this year, combining Bengal’s taste with local dairy richness. Visit our store for live mango pulp tasting sessions and take home our bestselling summer combo boxes.
